SUBJECT FOR RIP
42 BARCELONA

Allowed functions: puts, write.

Write a program that takes as an argument a string containing only parentheses. If the parentheses are unbalanced (e.g., "())"), your program shall remove the minimum number of parentheses in order for the expresion to be balanced.

By removing, we mean replacing them with spaces.
You will the print all the solutions (there may be more than one).

The order of the solutions does not matter.
